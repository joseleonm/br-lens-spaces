Bollobás–Riordan Polynomial of Lens Spaces

For each lens space L(p, q), the canonical Heegaard ribbon graph G(p; ±1, ±q) is embedded on the torus T². This database contains the full BR polynomial of G for all 45 homeomorphism orbits with 3 ≤ p ≤ 20. About

The file data/BR_polynomials.json contains, for each of the 45 canonical orbits of L(p, q) under orientation-preserving homeomorphism (3 ≤ p ≤ 20), the full list of monomials \(x^s y^t z^{2g}\) of BR(G(p; ±1, ±q)), together with the crest invariants S2 and m2. This work is part of a research programme associating polynomial invariants to 3-manifolds; for background see J. Frías, J. C. Gómez-Larrañaga, J. L. León-Medina, F. Manjarrez-Gutiérrez, “3-manifold polynomials,” arXiv:2510.06651, 2025.
